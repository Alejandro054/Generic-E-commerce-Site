About Us

Welcome to Retail Avenue, a place full of great deals and opportunity for customers to have a great life changing changing experience at an affordable price. Retail Avenue started spontaneously as a small idea to resell house hold items off a small garage. It is now a structured company trying to grow into a valuable Brand and an innovative movement. Retail Avenue is a community oriented space, we are open to any kind of feedback and product recommendations. Our mission is to help you have the experience of a lifetime while saving money in the process if there are any concerns or any ideas on how to make this better feel free to contact us.
